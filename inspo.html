Our Woman in Tech Inspiration: Stephanie Tilenius

About Her:

Stephanie Tilenius is the CEO and co-founder of VidaHealth, a mobile app platform that evidence-based resources to combat chronic diseases, through the use of personal coaches, clinicians, and trainers. Graduated from Brandeis University and Harvard University and was an executive at PayPal, eBay, and Google. We chose Stephanie Tilenius as our WiT Inspiration because her commitment to providing resources for wellbeing, similar to our project.
